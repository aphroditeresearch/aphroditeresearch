Research developments we're tracking — what changed, and what it means for the evidence. Verified before posting.

May 21, 2026 Metabolism

Retatrutide's first pivotal Phase 3 obesity trial reads out

TRIUMPH-1 (2,339 adults) reported up to ~28–30% average weight loss over 80 weeks. Strong human data — but still investigational, not approved, and the cardiovascular-outcomes trial runs for years yet.
Jul 23–24, 2026 Regulatory

FDA advisory committee to weigh BPC-157, TB-500, MOTS-c & Semax

The Pharmacy Compounding Advisory Committee is scheduled to discuss whether these bulk substances belong on the 503A compounding list — a live signal on their regulatory status.
Apr 2026 Regulatory

BPC-157, TB-500 & CJC-1295 left in a regulatory gray zone

FDA removed them from the Category 2 "safety-concern" list, but didn't approve them for compounding either. Neither prohibited nor authorized — and still not FDA-approved drugs.
2026 Skin

GHK-Cu interest surges; topical evidence still outpaces injectable

Search interest reportedly jumped ~1,000% year-over-year. The topical skin research is real and growing — but injectable "full-body" claims remain far ahead of the human data.
