Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: [DATE]. Review with a licensed attorney before publishing.

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs how you may interact with Aphrodite Research ("the Site"), including any comment, message, submission, or community feature we may offer. It exists to keep the Site strictly educational and to maintain a clear separation between published research and any real-world application.

The Core Rule

The Site discusses the published scientific literature on peptides and research compounds. It is not a place to discuss, request, describe, or facilitate real-world use. This applies to our own content and to anything you submit.

Prohibited Content and Conduct

You may not post, request, or solicit:

1. Human use. Any discussion of using a compound in or on a human being, including personal anecdotes of use.

2. Veterinary or animal use. Any discussion of administering a compound to an animal.

3. Dosing or protocols. Dosages, administration routes, cycles, schedules, or "how to use" instructions of any kind.

4. Sourcing or acquisition. Where to buy, sell, obtain, trade, or manufacture any compound; vendor names, links, reviews, prices, or availability.

5. Medical or veterinary advice. Requests for or provision of diagnosis, treatment, or health guidance.

6. Circumvention. Coded language, euphemisms, or indirect phrasing intended to elicit any of the above.

Permitted Content

  • Discussion of mechanisms of action as described in the literature.
  • Summaries, critiques, and comparisons of published studies.
  • Questions about study design, methodology, and interpretation.
  • News about research, trials, and regulatory developments.

Enforcement

We may remove any content, and suspend or terminate any account, that violates this AUP, at our discretion and without notice. Where community features exist, we moderate to remove prohibited content promptly. We are not obligated to publish any user submission.
