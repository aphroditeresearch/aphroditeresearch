# APHRODITE RESEARCH — COMPOUND INTAKE TEMPLATE

> **How to use this file.** Copy it, rename it `intake-[compound].md`, and fill every field.
> Anything in `[[ double brackets ]]` is an instruction to you — delete it once filled.
> When complete, the FREE fields populate `compound.html` and the DEEP-DIVE fields
> populate `deepdive.html`. One filled template = one full published entry.
>
> **The Examine.com model we're following:** read the primary source, summarize the
> *evidence* in plain language, rate how strong that evidence is, and separate what's
> proven from what's speculative. We are an evidence-adjudicator, not a promoter.

---

## ⚠️ NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES (read before every entry)

1. **Every factual claim traces to a cited primary source.** No claim without a citation.
2. **Paraphrase, never paste.** Summarize abstracts in your own words. No copied text.
3. **No dosing, no protocols, no routes, no sourcing.** Ever. Not even from the paper.
4. **Report what studies *observed under controlled conditions* — never what a person should expect.**
5. **Risks and unknowns get equal-or-greater prominence than effects.** This is the ethical + legal core.
6. **A human verifies every number against the primary source before publishing.** AI drafts; a person signs off.
7. **Separate human evidence from animal/in-vitro evidence explicitly.** Never blur them.
