How the Lens works

Retrieval, not guesswork

i.

You paste a claim

Any peptide claim, caption, or product line you've come across.

ii.

We match it

The claim is matched against our hand-reviewed evidence records — never answered from thin air.

iii.

You get a verdict

Human evidence, preclinical evidence, claim confidence, and the hype gap — separated, not blended.

iv.

You see the sources

Every verdict links to the evidence record behind it, so you can check our work.

Aphrodite Lens explains what published research has and hasn't shown. It does not give medical advice, dosing, administration, or sourcing guidance, and a verdict about the evidence is not a recommendation to use anything.
