Aphrodite Research is free, and the evidence stays open to everyone. We don't sell compounds, dosing, or sourcing — there's nothing to buy here but understanding. Support is optional and only helps us stay independent and keep the whole archive free.

Questions

Is it really all free?

Yes. Every verdict, evidence breakdown, citation, and tool is open to everyone with no account and no paywall. Supporting the project is optional and simply helps keep it independent and free.

Do you sell peptides or give dosing advice?

No. We never sell, supply, or broker any compound, and we provide no dosing, protocols, or sourcing — for anyone. This is an educational resource that reports what the published research found.

Why not just sell subscriptions?

Because the moment we profit from pushing people toward peptides, we become the thing we're trying to counter. Staying free and non-selling is the whole point — it's what makes the information trustworthy.

What does "support" get me?

Mostly the good feeling of funding independent research. Practically: early access to new dossiers and the weekly digest. It's a way to help, not a gate — nothing is locked behind it.
