Questions

What am I actually paying for?

The work of reading, sorting, and interpreting the published research — assembled into one place, kept current, and monitored so you learn when a verdict changes. The science itself is public; the synthesis and vigilance are the product.

Do you sell peptides or give dosing advice?

No. We never sell, supply, or broker any compound, and we provide no dosing, protocols, or sourcing — for members or anyone else. This is an educational resource only.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel whenever you like; access continues to the end of the billing period and you're never charged again.

Is the free tier actually useful?

Genuinely, yes. The verdict, Evidence Score, Claim Gap, and Route Integrity warnings are all free on every compound. Membership is for depth and monitoring, not for basic safety information.
